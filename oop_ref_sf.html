Final Reflection & Learning Outcomes

    Sommerville in (Sommerville, 2013) describes the difference between systematic (deterministic) and non-deterministic failures. Though, it should also be fairly said that over the last decade, the Internet of Things (IoT) has been arising and this idea came a bit too short in this book. So looking back to the Japanese ATM issue at the Mizuho Bank, it can also be a reason that the system failed because the system has been adapated to IoT over the last few years (month). For example using wifi, the ATM could easily connect to people around the ATM (to the smartphones for example when an open wifi network, the city network for example, is used) and therefore could simply try to estimate future ATM transactions. This is only one idea to think about IoT in terms how it could be an initializer to the system failure at the ATM. Nevertheless, this reason was not mentioned in the report that has been published by the bank itself in 2021 (Mizuho Financial Group, 2021). So, it seems unlikely that IoT adaptations played a role in this case.
